Wellness Buddy

Hi there, I'm a computer chatbot and part of a research project with the goal of assigning you to your preferred mental health training condition. I'll listen to your concerns, preference, and provide help to support your wellbeing and coping abilities.

I'm still under developing and will be soon published for research use :)

Development Tools: Python, JavaScript, & MongoDB

Self-Control & Decision Making

This project studied the impact of short-term versus long-term financial decision patterns making for people of varying incomes and ethnic backgrounds using an online survey I made in Qualtrics.

User Behavior Assessment

This online study was created with JavaScript, jsPsych, and Firebase to collect data on game players. Qualitative and quantitative data analysese were conducted to understand antecedents and consequences of user behaviours in Python.

This study was presented at the Association for Behavior Analysis (ABAI) 2020 Conference

Multilingual Mindfulness Training & Physical Activity

This project helped international students to improve exercise behaviours with the use of mindfulness training. Quantitative (e.g., steps, gym visits) and qualitative data (e.g., individual preferences, goal setting) were collected to write and analyze concurrent progress reports in Python.

This study was orally presented and published published at the Journal of Contextual Behavioral Science.
